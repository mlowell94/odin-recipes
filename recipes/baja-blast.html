Baja Blast

A version of the popular Taco Bell drink you can make and enjoy at home!

Ingredients

  • Blue Powerade
  • Mountain Dew
  • Lemon Lime Soda

Directions

  1. Fill 2 8 oz glasses halfway with ice.
  2. Add 3 oz of Powerade to each glass.
  3. Add 3 oz of Mountain Dew to each glass.
  4. Add 2 oz of lemon lime soda to each glass
  5. Stir to combine
